Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Unfortunatly we do not currently ship internationally, but we are hoping to in the near future, we currently do ship our products within South Africa.

Do you offer custom sizing or alterations?

We do not offer custom sizing or any alterations.

Do you offer gift cards?

We currently do not offer gift cards or loyalty programs but we look to offer these in the near future.

What is your return policy?

We allow returns within 30 days after delivery has taken place. Returns will include a full refund of the purchase.

How long does shipping take?

For deliveries within the Eastern Cape the expected delivery period is 3-5 days while for other provinces the expected period is 1-2 weeks.
